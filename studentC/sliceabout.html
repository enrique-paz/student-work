WELCOME TO SLICE

Slice is your neighborhood cafe with everything you need throughout the day to dine in or take away. We open early in the morning with fresh baked bread that we use to create our variety of savory and sweet toasts. In addition, we offer a full cafe menu which features a selection of espresso, tea, and juice beverages.

Slice is a team of 16 people, we ake over 400 loaves a day, serve toast and beverages every night. We use our mill to grind all of our whole grain flour every day, and use the ingredients that are fairly traded and locally sourced. We hope you love eating the bread as much as we love making it!
