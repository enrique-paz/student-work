Our Story

Doughnut Plant is an independent New York City based bakery that is considered the pioneer of the artisanal doughnut. They pride themselves on originality, using the highest quality all natural ingredients, and making everything in-house from scratch — all glazes, fillings, jams and toppings.

Created In

Created in Mark Isreal’s Lower East Side apartment in 1994, we have been handcrafting original and innovative doughnuts built upon Mark’s grandfather’s recipe ever since.

One-Man Band

For over five years, Mark was essentially a one-man band, spending his nights mixing, rolling, cutting, frying and glazing before delivering his creations by bicycle to specialty shops around New York City.

In 2000

In 2000, Doughnut Plant opened its first storefront on Grand Street in the Lower East Side. The passion for originality pours out into every detail of our shops, too.
